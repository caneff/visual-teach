Teacher Box

A standing reminder that the AI running the lesson is the learner's teacher — inviting them to ask follow-up questions whenever something is unclear.

Example

I'm your teacher — ask me anything

If something here didn't land, send me a follow-up question and I'll work through it with you. No question is too small. A few you might start with:

Can you explain this with a different example? Why does this work better than the alternative? What should I learn next to go deeper?

Minimal variant

Stuck? Just ask

I'm your teacher for this lesson — ask me to clarify anything that didn't make sense, in your own words.
