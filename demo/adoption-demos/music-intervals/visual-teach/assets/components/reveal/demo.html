What two parents does a merge commit have, and why?
The tip of each branch being merged. The merge commit records both histories converging, so it points back at both.
Why do rebased commits get new hashes?
A hash is derived from the commit's content plus its parent. Replay gives each commit a new parent, so it's a new commit with a new hash.
