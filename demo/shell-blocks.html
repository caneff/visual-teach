Turn raw columns into the numbers your dashboard actually needs.

⏱ ~15 min Needs: a report from Lesson 1 Intermediate Lesson 3 of 9

By the end of this lesson you'll be able to:

  • Create a calculated field from two existing columns
  • Tell the difference between row-level and aggregate calculations
  • Spot when a ratio is being computed at the wrong granularity
Why this matters for your goal: your internal BI transfers need per-capita figures, and those don't exist as columns — you'll compute them here.

1The idea#

A calculated field is a formula evaluated per row or per aggregate. You compose it visually; Data Studio writes the expression.
